Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 13, 2025

DIRTTIME ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our DIRTTIME mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

When you use DIRTTIME, you may provide the following information:

  • Bike Information: Details about your motorcycles including make, model, year, current hours, and mileage
  • Maintenance Records: Service logs, maintenance tasks, and completion dates
  • Ride Logs: Information about your rides including hours ridden, distance traveled, and terrain type
  • Photos: Images you choose to associate with your bikes

Automatically Collected Information

DIRTTIME does not automatically collect personal information, analytics data, or usage statistics. We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.

Data Storage

Local Storage Only

All your data is stored locally on your device. DIRTTIME does not transmit your bike information, maintenance records, ride logs, or photos to any external servers. Your data remains entirely on your iPhone or iPad.

We use Apple's built-in storage systems (AsyncStorage and App Groups for widget functionality) to securely store your data on your device.

No Cloud Sync

DIRTTIME does not currently offer cloud synchronization. This means:

  • Your data is not backed up to our servers
  • We cannot access, view, or recover your data
  • Your data stays private and under your control

Important: We recommend using your device's built-in backup features (iCloud Backup or iTunes/Finder backup) to protect against data loss.

Third-Party Services

DIRTTIME does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics providers
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Crash reporting services

Device Permissions

DIRTTIME may request the following permissions:

  • Camera/Photo Library: Used only if you choose to add photos of your bikes. Photos are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared.

All permissions are optional and the app functions fully without granting them.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, security is provided by:

  • Apple's iOS security features and encryption
  • Your device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID protection
  • App sandboxing that prevents other apps from accessing DIRTTIME's data

Children's Privacy

DIRTTIME is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since all data is stored locally and never transmitted, no children's data is collected by us.

Your Rights

Data Access and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app at any time
  • Modification: You can edit or update any information in the app
  • Deletion: You can delete individual bikes, logs, or tasks within the app. To delete all data, simply uninstall the app from your device.
  • Export: Your data remains on your device and can be preserved through iOS backups

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Your continued use of the App after any modifications indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: support@dirttime.xyz

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights regarding your personal information under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Since DIRTTIME stores all data locally on your device and does not collect, sell, or share personal information with third parties, these rights are inherently protected by our privacy-first design.

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Because DIRTTIME does not collect or process personal data on external servers, and all information remains on your device, you maintain full control over your data at all times.
