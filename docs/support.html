Frequently Asked Questions

Tap the "+" button on the Garage screen to add a new bike. Enter your bike's details including make, model, year, and current hours/mileage. You can also add a photo to help identify your bike at a glance.

DIRTTIME supports four types of maintenance intervals:

  • Hours: Service is due after a certain number of engine hours (e.g., oil change every 15 hours)
  • Distance: Service is due after traveling a certain distance in km or miles
  • Days: Service is due after a certain number of days (e.g., annual inspections)
  • Per Ride: Tasks that should be checked before or after every ride

You can customize intervals based on your owner's manual or riding conditions.

DIRTTIME uses three status indicators:

  • OK (Green): Service is not yet due. You're good to ride!
  • Due Soon (Yellow): Service will be due soon (within 90% of the interval). Plan to service soon.
  • Overdue (Red): Service is past due. Address this before your next ride.

Riding in harsh conditions wears on your bike more than normal trail riding. DIRTTIME adjusts maintenance calculations based on terrain:

  • Sand: 70% multiplier (1 hour in sand = 1.4 hours of wear)
  • Mud & Water: 80% multiplier (1 hour = 1.25 hours of wear)
  • Racing: 85% multiplier (1 hour = 1.17 hours of wear)

This helps ensure you service your bike more frequently when riding in demanding conditions.

DIRTTIME includes iOS widgets that show your bike's maintenance status at a glance:

  1. Long-press on your home screen until apps jiggle
  2. Tap the "+" button in the top-left corner
  3. Search for "DIRTTIME" or scroll to find it
  4. Choose your preferred widget size
  5. Tap "Add Widget"

The widget will automatically update to show maintenance status for your bikes.

DIRTTIME stores all data locally on your device. To protect against data loss:

  • Enable iCloud Backup on your device (Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > iCloud Backup)
  • Or regularly back up to your computer using Finder (Mac) or iTunes (Windows)

When you restore your device from a backup, your DIRTTIME data will be restored too.

Tips & Troubleshooting

App running slowly?

Try closing and reopening the app. If issues persist, restarting your device often helps. Make sure you're running the latest version of iOS and DIRTTIME.

Widget not updating?

Widgets refresh periodically based on iOS guidelines. Open the main app to force a data sync with the widget. If the widget still shows old data, try removing and re-adding it.

Want to suggest a feature?

We're always looking to improve! Send your feature requests to support@dirttime.xyz. We read every email and prioritize features based on user feedback.

Found a bug?

Please report bugs to support@dirttime.xyz with:

  • What you were doing when the bug occurred
  • What you expected to happen
  • What actually happened
  • Your device model and iOS version

Screenshots are always helpful!

Best practices for accurate tracking

  • Log rides immediately after completing them for the most accurate tracking
  • Use an hour meter on your bike for precise engine hour tracking
  • Set up all maintenance tasks before logging your first ride
  • Mark services complete as soon as you perform them
  • Review and adjust intervals based on your specific bike and conditions
